1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Protect the UNBORN (Undo the Negligent Biden Orders Right Now) Act or the Protect the UNBORN Act. 2. Prohibitions on implementation of Executive order relating to reproductive health services
(a) Federal agencies
No head of a Federal agency may carry out—
(1) Executive Order 14076 entitled Protecting Access to Reproductive Healthcare Services and issued on July 8, 2022; or
(2) Executive Order 14079 entitled Securing Access to Reproductive and Other Healthcare Services and issued on August 3, 2022.(b) Use of funds
No Federal funds, including any funds made available under the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2022 (Public Law 117–103), may be used to implement, administer, or enforce—
(1) Executive Order 14076 entitled Protecting Access to Reproductive Healthcare Services and issued on July 8, 2022; or
(2) Executive Order 14079 entitled Securing Access to Reproductive and Other Healthcare Services and issued on August 3, 2022.
