1. Alan S. Lowenthal Post Office Building
(a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 300 Long Beach Boulevard in Long Beach, California, shall be known and designated as the Alan S. Lowenthal Post Office Building.(b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Alan S. Lowenthal Post Office Building.
