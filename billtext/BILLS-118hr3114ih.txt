1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Town Halls for Veterans Act. 2. Department of Veterans Affairs town hall meetings at medical centers
The Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall provide for quarterly town hall meetings for veterans at each medical center of the Department. Each such meeting shall include the director of the medical center, or the director’s designee, and the director of the Veterans Integrated Service Network in which the medical center is located, or the director’s designee, and other appropriate employees of the Department, as determined by the Secretary.
