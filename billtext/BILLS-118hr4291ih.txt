
1. ZIP Code modification for Goose Creek, South Carolina
Not later than 45 days after the date of enactment of this section, the United States Postal Service shall modify the boundaries of the ZIP Code applicable to Goose Creek, South Carolina, to conform with the boundaries set forth in the map titled ZIP Code Boundaries Proposal 1, dated June 7, 2023, published by the City of Goose Greek GIS Department.
