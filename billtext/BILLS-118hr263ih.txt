
1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Stop Trying to Obsessively Vilify Energy Act or the STOVE Act. 2. Prohibition on promulgating any rule or guidance that bans gas stoves in the United States
No Federal agency may propose, implement, or finalize a rule or guidance that restricts or bans the use and purchase of gas-powered stoves, cooktops, ranges, or ovens in the United States.
