1. Limitation on availability of funds for US Department of Agriculture, Under Secretary for Marketing and Regulatory Programs, APHIS, Salaries and Expenses for fiscal year 2024
 Notwithstanding any other provision of the law, amounts authorized to be appropriated or otherwise available for US Department of Agriculture, Under Secretary for Marketing and Regulatory Programs, APHIS, Salaries and Expenses for fiscal year 2024 may not exceed $1,011,136,000.
