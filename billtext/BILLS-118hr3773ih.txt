1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Stop Anti-Semitism on College Campuses Act.2. Prohibition of antisemitic events on campus
Section 487(a) of the Higher Education Act of 1965 (20 U.S.C. 1094(a)) is amended by adding at the end the following: 
(30) (A) The institution will not authorize, facilitate, provide funding for, or otherwise support any event promoting Anti-Semitism on campus.(B) In this paragraph, the term Anti-Semitism has the meaning given the working definition of Anti-Semitism adopted by the International Holocaust Remembrance Alliance on May 26, 2016, including the contemporary examples of Anti-Semitism cited by the Alliance..
