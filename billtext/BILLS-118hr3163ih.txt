
1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the End Judge Shopping Act of 2023. 2. Orders purporting to restrain enforcement against non-parties
 (a) In general
Chapter 87 of title 28, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:

1414. Civil actions seeking nationwide injunctions
A civil action which seeks an order enforceable in each district and division of the United States shall be brought only in a division of a judicial district which has two or more active judges assigned.. (b) Clerical amendment
The table of sections for such chapter is amended by adding at the end the following:


1414. Civil actions seeking nationwide injunctions..
