
1. Dosan Ahn Chang Ho Post Office

(a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 3500 West 6th Street, Suite 103 in Los Angeles, California, shall be known and designated as the Dosan Ahn Chang Ho Post Office. (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Dosan Ahn Chang Ho Post Office.
