1. Lucille Roybal-Allard Post Office Building
(a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 7911 Imperial Highway in Downey, California, shall be known and designated as the Lucille Roybal-Allard Post Office Building.(b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Lucille Roybal-Allard Post Office Building.
