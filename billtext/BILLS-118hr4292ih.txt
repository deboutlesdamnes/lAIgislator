
1. Laura Matilda Towne Post Office Building

(a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 774 Sea Island Pkwy, in Saint Helena Island, South Carolina, shall be known and designated as the Laura Matilda Towne Post Office Building. (b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Laura Matilda Towne Post Office Building.
