
1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Medical Device Electronic Labeling Act. 2. Allowing required labeling of devices to be made available solely by electronic means
Section 502(f) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (21 U.S.C. 352(f)) is amended by striking prescription devices intended for use in health care facilities or by a health care professional and required labeling for in vitro diagnostic devices intended for use by health care professionals or in blood establishments and inserting devices (including in vitro diagnostic devices).
