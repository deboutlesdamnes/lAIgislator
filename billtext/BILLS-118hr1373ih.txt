1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Stop Adversarial Funding Expenditures for Laboratories Act or the SAFE Laboratories Act. 2. Prohibition on assistance to Wuhan Institute of Virology and other foreign laboratories
No head of a Federal agency may provide assistance, directly or indirectly, to the Wuhan Institute of Virology, or any other laboratory located in a country determined by the Secretary of State to be a foreign adversary, including China, Russia, North Korea, and Iran.
