1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Protect Vulnerable Immigrant Youth Act. 2. Eliminating certain employment-based visa caps
(a) Aliens Not Subject to Direct Numerical Limitations
Section 201(b)(1)(A) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (8 U.S.C. 1151(b)(1)(A)) is amended by striking subparagraph (A) or (B) and inserting subparagraph (A), (B), or (J).(b) Preference Allocation for Employment-Based Immigrants
Section 203(b)(4) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (8 U.S.C. 1153(b)(4)) is amended by striking subparagraph (A) or (B) and inserting subparagraph (A), (B), or (J).
