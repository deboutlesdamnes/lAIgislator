
1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the No Altering Public Comments Act. 2. Prohibition from deleting public comments on agency websites
 (a) Prohibition
An agency may not remove or alter any public comment submitted to or published on the website of an agency unless such comment is threatening or harms national security.
(b) Agency defined
In this section, the term agency has the meaning given that term in section 551 of title 5, United States Code.
