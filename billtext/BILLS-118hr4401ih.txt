1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Restoring Earned Veterans Benefits Act.2. Payment of certain veterans compensation after discontinuance by reason of receipt of active service pay
(a) Timing of resumption of payment

(1) Establishment
Subchapter II of chapter 51 of title 38, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following new section:5114. Resumption of period of payment after discontinuance by reason of receipt of active service pay
Payment of compensation or pension, discontinued by reason of receipt of active service pay (pursuant to subsection (c) of section 5304 of this title), shall resume on the first day after the period of active service. The Secretary may resume such payment if the veteran has not filed a claim for such payment if the Secretary determines that the record contains sufficient evidence to re-establish the entitlement of the veteran to such benefit. .
(2) Clerical amendment
The table of sections at the beginning of such chapter is amended by inserting after the item relating to section 5113 the following new item: 5114. Resumption of period of payment after discontinuance by reason of receipt of active service pay..(b) Implementation plan
