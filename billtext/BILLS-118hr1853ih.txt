
1. Limitation on availability of funds for Senate, Offices of the Secretaries of the Conference of the Majority and the Conference of the Minority for fisal year 2024
Notwithstanding any other provision of the law, amounts authorized to be appropriated or otherwise available for Senate, Offices of the Secretaries of the Conference of the Majority and the Conference of the Minority for fiscal year 2024 may not exceed $843,402.
