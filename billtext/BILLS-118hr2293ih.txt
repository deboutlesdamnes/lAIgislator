1. Limitation on availability of funds for US Department of Agriculture, Foreign Assistance and Related Programs, McGovern-Dole International Food for Education and Child Nutrition Program Grants for fiscal year 2024
Notwithstanding any other provision of the law, amounts authorized to be appropriated or otherwise available for US Department of Agriculture, Foreign Assistance and Related Programs, McGovern-Dole International Food for Education and Child Nutrition Program Grants for fiscal year 2024 may not exceed $210,255,000.
