This Act may be cited as the Drought Preparedness Act.2. Extension of authorization
(a) Drought program
Section 104(c) of the Reclamation States Emergency Drought Relief Act of 1991 (43 U.S.C. 2214(c)) is amended by striking 2022 and inserting 2028.(b) Authorization of appropriations
Section 301 of the Reclamation States Emergency Drought Relief Act of 1991 (43 U.S.C. 2241) is amended by striking 2022 and inserting 2028.
