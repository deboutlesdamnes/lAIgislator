(1) in paragraph (1), by striking , revocation, or limitation and inserting or revocation; and
(2) in paragraph (2)—(A) by striking may revoke, restrict, or limit and inserting revoke; and(B) by inserting , and notify such individual of the taking of any such action before the period.
