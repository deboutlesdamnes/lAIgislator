1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Retroactive Foreign Agents Registration Act.2. Clarifying obligation to register retroactively as agents of foreign principals
(a) Obligation
The third sentence of section 2(a) of the Foreign Agents Registration Act of 1938, as amended (22 U.S.C. 612(a)) is amended by striking for the period and inserting covering the period.(b) Effective date
The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply with respect to any individual who serves as the agent of a foreign principal under the Foreign Agents Registration Act of 1938, as amended, at any time before, on, or after the date of the enactment of this Act.3.
