
1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Fairness in Medicare Savings Program Act of 2023.  2. Expanding the Medicare Savings Program to the territories
 (a) In general
Section 1905(p)(4) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1396d(p)(4)) is amended by inserting for fiscal years through 2023 after Columbia).
(b) Nonapplication of funding cap
Section 1108(g)(4) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1308(g)(4)) is amended by adding at the end the following new subparagraph:  (C) Exclusion relating to Medicare cost-sharing
Payments under section 1903 for medical assistance consisting of medicare cost-sharing (as defined in section 1905(p)(3)) furnished on or after October 1, 2023, shall not be taken into account in applying any limit under subsection (f) or this subsection..
