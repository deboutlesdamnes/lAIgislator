
1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Non-Recognition of Russian Annexation of Ukrainian Territory Act. 2. Prohibition against United States recognition of the Russian Federation’s claim of sovereignty over any portion of Ukraine
 (a) Statement of policy
It is the policy of the United States not to recognize the Russian Federation’s claim of sovereignty over any portion of the internationally recognized territory of Ukraine, including its airspace and its territorial waters.
(b) Prohibition
In accordance with subsection (a), no Federal department or agency may take any action or extend any assistance that implies recognition of the Russian Federation’s claim of sovereignty over any portion of the internationally recognized territory of Ukraine, including its airspace and its territorial waters.
