1. Pamela Jane Rock Post Office Building
(a) Designation
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 859 North State Road 21 in Melrose, Florida, shall be known and designated as the Pamela Jane Rock Post Office Building.(b) References
Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the Pamela Jane Rock Post Office Building.
