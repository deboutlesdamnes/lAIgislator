1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the REAL ID Eligibility for Ukraine Act or the RIDE for Ukraine Act. 2. REAL ID benefits
Section 401(b) of the Additional Ukraine Supplemental Appropriations Act, 2022 (8 U.S.C. 1101 note(b)) is amended—
(1) in paragraph (1), by striking and at the end;
(2) in paragraph (2), by striking the period at the end and inserting ; and; and
(3) by adding at the end the following:
(3) a driver’s license or identification card under section 202 of the REAL ID Act of 2005 (division B of Public Law 109–13; U.S.C. 30301 note), notwithstanding subsection (c)(2)(B) of such Act..
