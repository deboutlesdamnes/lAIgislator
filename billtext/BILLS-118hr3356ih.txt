
1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Motor Carrier Safety Screening Modernization Act. 2. Safety performance history screening
Section 31150 of title 49, United States Code, is amended—

(1) in subsection (a) by striking preemployment and inserting employment; and 
(2) in subsection (c)—
(A) by striking a preemployment and inserting an employment; and (B) by striking the preemployment and inserting the employment.
