1. Establishing new ZIP Codes
Not later than 270 days after the date of enactment of this Act, the United States Postal Service shall designate a single, unique ZIP Code for each of the following communities:
(1) Miami Lakes, Florida.
(2) Hollywood, Florida.
(3) Village of Somers, Wisconsin.
(4) Village of Mount Pleasant, Wisconsin.
(5) Village of Caledonia, Wisconsin.
(6) Eastvale, California.
(7) Castle Pines, Colorado.
