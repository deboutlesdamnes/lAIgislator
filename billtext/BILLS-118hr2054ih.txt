1. Limitation on availability of funds for Department of Treasury, Office of the Special Inspector General for the Troubled Asset Relief Program, Salaries and Expenses for fiscal year 2024
Notwithstanding any other provision of the law, amounts authorized to be appropriated or otherwise available for Department of Treasury, Office of the Special Inspector General for the Troubled Asset Relief Program, Salaries and Expenses for fiscal year 2024 may not exceed $16,000,000.
