1. Limitation on availability of funds for U.S. Department of Interior, Natural Resource Damage Assessment and Restoration, Natural Resource Damage Assessment Fund for fiscal year 2024
Notwithstanding any other provision of the law, amounts authorized to be appropriated or otherwise available for U.S. Department of Interior, Natural Resource Damage Assessment and Restoration, Natural Resource Damage Assessment Fund for fiscal year 2024 may not exceed $7,767,000.
