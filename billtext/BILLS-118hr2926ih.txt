
1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Race Horse Cost Recovery Act of 2023. 2. Three-year depreciation for race horses
 (a) In general
Section 168(e)(3)(A)(i) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended to read as follows:

(i) any race horse,. (b) Effective date
The amendments made by this section shall apply to property placed in service after December 31, 2020.
