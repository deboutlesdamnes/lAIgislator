213. Water conservation measures in the Great Salt Lake basin

(a) In general
 The Secretary may use any unexpended budget authority provided under subsections (a)(2) and (c) of section 202 for the conduct of water conservation measures within the Great Salt Lake basin in accordance with section 207. (b) Compliance with Definite Plan Report
