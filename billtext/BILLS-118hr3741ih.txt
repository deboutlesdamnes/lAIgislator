
1. Prohibition on title X funding for systems through which counseling or referrals are provided to individuals regarding abortion services
Title X of the Public Health Service Act (42 U.S.C. 300 et seq.) is amended by adding at the end the following:  1009. Prohibition on systems through which counseling or referrals are provided to individuals regarding abortion services
 (a) Prohibition
Except as provided in subsection (b), none of the funds appropriated under this title shall be used for a system, such as a hotline or website, through which counseling or referrals are provided to individuals regarding abortion services.
(b) Exceptions
