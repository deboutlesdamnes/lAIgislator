1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Preventing Improper Payments Act. 2. Treatment of certain new programs and activities
Section 3352(a) of title 31, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following: 
(4) Rule of construction
Any program or activity established by a Federal agency on or after the date of the enactment of this paragraph making more than $100,000,000 in payments in a fiscal year shall be construed to be a program or activity with outlays exceeding the statutory threshold dollar amount described in paragraph (3)(A)(i) that may be susceptible to significant improper payment..3.
