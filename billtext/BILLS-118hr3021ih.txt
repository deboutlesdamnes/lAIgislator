
1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Protecting the Second Amendment in Financial Services Act. 2. Merchant category code limitations with respect to firearm merchants
Section 127 of the Truth in Lending Act (15 U.S.C. 1637) is amended by inserting after subsection (p) the following:

(q) Merchant category code limitations with respect to firearm merchants


(1) In general
A covered entity may not use a merchant category code that separately identifies firearms merchants or ammunition merchants.
