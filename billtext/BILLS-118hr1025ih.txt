1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Defund the Wuhan Institute of Virology Act.2. Prohibition on Federal funding to the Wuhan Institute of Virology
(a) In general
No funds authorized or appropriated by Federal law may be made available to the Wuhan Institute of Virology for any purpose.(b) GAO study and report
Not later than 2 years after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Comptroller General of the United States shall conduct a study and submit a report to Congress regarding the amount of Federal funds that were awarded or indirectly provided (whether purposely or inadvertently) during the 15-year period immediately preceding such date of enactment to—
(1) the Wuhan Institute of Virology, including affiliated researchers;
(2) the People’s Republic of China;
(3) the Chinese Communist Party; or 
(4) any agency or instrumentality of the entities listed in paragraphs (1) through (3).
