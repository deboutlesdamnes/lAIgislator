
1. Timber block grant program

(a) In general
Not later than 90 days after the date of the enactment of this Act, the Secretary of Agriculture shall make available $80,000,000, to be derived from the unobligated balances of amounts made available under the heading Department of Agriculture—Farm Service Agency—Emergency Forest Restoration Program under title I of the Additional Supplemental Appropriations Act, 2019 (Public Law 116–20), to make a block grant to the State of Florida to carry out activities to address timber losses in such State due to Hurricanes Michael and Florence.  (b) Conditions
Funds made available pursuant to the block grant required under subsection (a) may be used to provide grants to—

(1) qualified land owners in the State of Florida to carry out activities to address timber losses on less than or equal to 10,000 acres of the land of each such landowner; and 
(2) any landowner described in paragraph (1) that was previously disqualified for a grant due to limited timber crop-loss insurance and that otherwise is a qualified landowner.
