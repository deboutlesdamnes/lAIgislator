1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Strengthening Community Care Act of 2023. 2. Extension for community health centers and the National Health Service Corps
(a) Community health centers
Section 10503(b)(1)(F) of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act (42 U.S.C. 254b–2(b)(1)(F)) is amended by striking 2023 and inserting 2028. (b) National Health Service Corps
Section 10503(b)(2)(H) of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act (42 U.S.C. 254b–2(b)(2)(H)) is amended by striking 2023 and inserting 2028.
