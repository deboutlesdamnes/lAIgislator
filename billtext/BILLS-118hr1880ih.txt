
1. Limitation on availability of funds for House of Representatives, Allowance for Compensation of Interns in House Appropriations Committee Offices for fiscal year 2024
Notwithstanding any other provision of the law, amounts authorized to be appropriated or otherwise available for House of Representatives, Allowance for Compensation of Interns in House Appropriations Committee Offices for fiscal year 2024 may not exceed $345,584.
