1. Limitation on availability of funds for US Department of Agriculture, Agricultural Marketing Service, Funds for Strengthening Markets, Income, and Supply (Section 32) for fiscal year 2024
Notwithstanding any other provision of the law, amounts authorized to be appropriated or otherwise available for US Department of Agriculture, Agricultural Marketing Service, Funds for Strengthening Markets, Income, and Supply (Section 32) for fiscal year 2024 may not exceed $20,705,000.
