
1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Multiple Firearm Sales Reporting Modernization Act of 2023. 2. Expansion of Federal firearm licensee multiple sales reporting requirement to all firearms
Section 923(g)(3)(A) of title 18, United States Code, is amended by striking pistols, or revolvers, or any combination of pistols and revolvers totalling two or more, and inserting firearms.
