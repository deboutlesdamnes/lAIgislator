1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Streamlining Aviation for Eligible Veterans Act or the SAFE Veterans Act.2. Inclusion of non-degree flight training in certain rehabilitation programs for certain veterans with service-connected disabilities
Section 3101 of title 38, United States Code, is amended in paragraph (6) by adding at the end the following: A rehabilitation program may include a program that includes flight training and does not lead to a degree..
