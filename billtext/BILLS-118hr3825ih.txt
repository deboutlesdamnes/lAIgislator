
1. Prohibition on use of funds for adult cabaret performances

(a) Prohibition
None of the funds appropriated or otherwise made available for the Department of Defense and no facilities owned or operated by Department of Defense may be used to host, advertise, or otherwise support an adult cabaret performance.  (b) Adult cabaret performance defined
In this section, the term adult cabaret performance means a performance that features topless dancers, go-go dancers, exotic dances, strippers, or male or female impersonators who provide entertainment that appeals to prurient interest.
