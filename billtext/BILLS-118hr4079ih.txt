1. Short title
This Act may be cited as the Substance Use Disorder Treatment and Recovery Loan Repayment Program Reauthorization Act of 2023. 2. Loan repayment program for the substance use disorder treatment workforce
Section 781(j) of the Public Health Service Act (42 U.S.C. 295h(j)) is amended by striking $25,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2019 through 2023 and inserting $40,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2024 through 2028.
