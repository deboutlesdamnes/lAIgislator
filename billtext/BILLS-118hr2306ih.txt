1. Limitation on availability of funds for Department of State, Administration of Foreign Affairs, Repatriations Loans Program Account, Direct Loans for fiscal year 2024
Notwithstanding any other provision of the law, amounts authorized to be appropriated or otherwise available for Department of State, Administration of Foreign Affairs, Repatriations Loans Program Account, Direct Loans for fiscal year 2024 may not exceed $1,300,000.
